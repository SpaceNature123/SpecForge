Who we are?

We are a small group of friends, aiming to create a website for people to have their dream PC. We found that many people gets confused by the amount of possibilities that one could build a PC or buy a laptop. So, we are here to make it less overwhelming, by curating the parts for you. We are planning to add many more things in future. Stay updated!


Our Aim and Mission

Our aim is to make PC building and laptop buying less overwhelming. No more headaches about which parts to buy and not. Our target is mainly gamers and those people who are new to computers who wants their computer to just work. But feel free to use this website, because you can learn many things from here
